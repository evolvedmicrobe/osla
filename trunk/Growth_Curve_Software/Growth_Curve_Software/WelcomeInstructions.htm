Welcome

 

The robot manager software is starting up and should be ready shortly.  Please wait while the instruments are initialized.

 

It is absolutely necessary that both computers in the room have a user logged onto them.

 

Please be aware of how much time remains before the next protocol instruction begins when moving plates in and out of the incubator.

 

  Do not attempt any commands from the incubator control tab until your last command has finished.

 

In the event that any error messages appear during or immediately after the instrument initialization, your best bet is to close the program and reopen it.  This usually indicates that the robot resources were not disposed of correctly, and  9 out of 10 times the program will catch the error and recover from it properly when it closes, allowing a clean start the next time.  For more sophisticated troubleshooting, see the “Recover from Errors” tab.
